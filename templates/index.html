Sentiment Analysis of the Airline Twitter

What is this about?

Twitter can sometimes have no mercy. But because of that, it is a wonderfully fast litmus test for tracking the customer experience.

We decided to look into Twitter superpowers for analyzing the user experience & brand image within the US airlines industry. On the subset of tweets from February 2015, which are mentioning any of the major 6 US airlines (American, Delta, Southwest, United, US Airways, Virgin) we're builduing a machine learning model which could then be used for fast sentiment analysis at any time. And we are also explaining the relationships in between the collected data.
